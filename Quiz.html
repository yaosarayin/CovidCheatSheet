I am Scared or Confused
It is totally normal to feel this way! This is a Global Pandemic that is affecting EVERYONE in the world. You might not know too much about the virus or what is happening, leading to fear and confusion.

Know that children are likely to exhibit more mild symptoms, there is no need to freak out, but we HAVE TO BE CAREFUL

You HAVE THE POWER to do the right things and stay informed to keep you and your loved ones safe! Washing your hands often and staying at home are both extremely effective ways to keep the virus away.
